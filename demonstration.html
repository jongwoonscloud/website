Product Demonstration
The "Waste-O" app has been selected as the issue's chosen project. The group has chosen it because it is modern, simple to use, and can be available for many people. Waste and recycling apps can be a great tool for communicating with, educating, and even entertaining your customers or other constituents (Bothwell, 2020). Essentially, the proposed "Waste-O" app has a design that can assist people in managing their waste better. Its main goal is to provide various facts, tips, and methods for promoting proper waste management. Furthermore, it also aims to help prevent climate change and global warming and its possible long-term effects.
